# Customer profitability

## Realized lifetime value

```
total FINALIZED company net profit from the customer
  - directly attributed acquisition cost
  = realized customer value
```

Realized, from closed moves. Any projected figure must be labelled a forecast;
none is produced in Stage 3.

## Reported

Bookings - completed moves - finalized moves - total billed - total collected -
refunds - direct costs - company net profit - average margin - outstanding
balance - acquisition source - acquisition cost (campaign cost-per-booking) -
repeat status.

Aggregation reuses `aggregateMoves()`, so a customer total can never disagree
with the company total.
